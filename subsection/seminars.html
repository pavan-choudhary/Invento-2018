Seminars

Invento is aimed to light up the young dwelling minds filled with the curiosity and spread the words of science and technology to every possible corner. Every year, the biggest auditorium of the city witnesses thousands of enthusiastic students during this 2 day thrilling mega event.

Last year, with footfall of over 7000 students from all around the central India, Invento organized various seminars and brought some of well pronounced societies to Indore for the first time. With the participation of Microsoft Technical Community and Mozilla, we had seminars on Ethical Hacking, Adobe, Robotronixx and many more.

Again, this year we are back with seminars by established educational institutes of city to enlighten on some most trending topics of technology.
